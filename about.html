About White Dentistry

Meet Dr. Patel

Dr. Patel believes in providing her patients with more than just world-class dental care. She also helps patients recognize the vital connection between dental health and whole body health. A graduate of the University of California’s School of Dentistry, Dr. Patel is a leader in the movement to bring environmental sanity and well-being into the dental world.

From the spa like comforts to high-tech non-invasive treatments, every aspect of her practice is thoughtfully designed and focused on cosmetics, comfort, and your lifelong health.
